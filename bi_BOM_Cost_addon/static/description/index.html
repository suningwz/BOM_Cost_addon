Process Costing in Manufacturing

Process Costing in Manufacturing module is used to do process costing (Material Cost, Labour Cost, Overheads Cost) for manufacturing orders.

Process Costing in Manufacturing


Bill of Materials - Direct Material Cost.

You can configure Direct Material Cost on Bill of Materials.


Bill of Materials - Direct Labour Cost.

You can configure Direct Labour Cost on Bill of Materials.


Bill of Materials - Direct Overhead Cost.

You can configure Direct Overhead Cost on Bill of Materials.




Create Manufacturing Orders

When you create manufacturing orders from BOM then selected BOM's Direct Material Cost, Direct Labour Cost and Direct Overhead Cost will be filled automatically on manufacturing order.






Manufacturing Orders Costing.

Here you can see total cost of Material,Labour and Overhead.


Create Manufacturing Workorder.

By clicking on Create Workorders button,Workorder will be created with Direct Material Cost, Direct Labour Cost and Direct Overhead Cost.




Workorder.

Click on work orders smart button to view created work orders.
